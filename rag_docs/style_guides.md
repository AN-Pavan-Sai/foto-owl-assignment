# Cinematic Wedding
A cinematic wedding style focuses on emotional resonance, slow pacing, and elegant presentation.
- Pacing: Slow and deliberate. Use long scene durations (e.g., 90-120 frames per image).
- Transitions: Subtle cross-fades or slow dissolves. Avoid harsh cuts.
- Color Tone: Warm, golden hour lighting.
- Captions: Minimal text. Use elegant serif fonts, positioned cleanly (e.g., bottom-center).
- Animations: Slow 'zoom-in' (Ken Burns effect) to emphasize emotion.

# Upbeat Birthday
An upbeat birthday style is energetic, fast-paced, and fun.
- Pacing: Fast cuts. Short scene durations (e.g., 30-45 frames per image).
- Transitions: Hard cuts, quick wipes, or bouncy spring transitions.
- Color Tone: Vibrant, high contrast, colorful.
- Captions: Bold, large text, often with background highlighting. Centered or dynamic placement.
- Animations: Fast pans, zoom-outs, or playful bounces.

# Corporate Highlights
A corporate highlights style is professional, clean, and straightforward.
- Pacing: Medium pacing, steady rhythm (e.g., 60-90 frames per image).
- Transitions: Clean wipes or short fades.
- Color Tone: Neutral, balanced, high clarity.
- Captions: Professional sans-serif fonts, lower-thirds placement. Clear and readable.
- Animations: Very subtle pan (left or right) to keep the frame slightly dynamic but not distracting.
